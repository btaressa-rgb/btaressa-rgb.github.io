About Me

Yodit Taressa

Information Systems Technology student · Web Designer · Digital Creator

My Story

Hello, I'm Yodit.

I'm an Information Systems Technology student at Northern Virginia Community College (NOVA), Annandale campus. My journey into technology started with a curiosity about how digital experiences are built — and that curiosity has grown into a genuine passion for web design, multimedia, and creative technology solutions.

I believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Whether I'm building a responsive website, editing a video, or organizing a digital project, I bring the same commitment: make it work beautifully for every user, on every device.

Outside of coursework, I'm drawn to the intersection of creativity and technology — where design thinking meets real-world problem-solving. I enjoy customer service, project organization, and finding digital solutions that make people's lives easier.
